Discover Ilocos Sur

Explore heritage, adventures, and local treasures.

Vigan Calle Crisologo

Walk along Calle Crisologo, lined with preserved Spanish-era houses and cobblestone streets. Experience calesa rides and local crafts. A UNESCO World Heritage Site.

#Vigan #Heritage #CalleCrisologo #Culture #UNESCO

Santiago Sabangan Beach

Santiago Sabangan Beach in Ilocos Sur is a peaceful spot with white sand, clear waters, and stunning sunsets. It’s perfect for swimming, relaxing, and enjoying fresh seafood by the shore.

#Santiago #SabanganBeach #Summer #Viewpoint

Bantay Bell Tower

An iconic watchtower with panoramic views of Vigan. It played a role in history as a lookout post. Climb up and enjoy the view with friends and family.

#Bantay #BellTower #History #Views #Photography
